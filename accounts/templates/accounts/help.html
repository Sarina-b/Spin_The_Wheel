Help & Guide

Welcome to Spin to Win! Here, you can create your own custom wheel by entering choices in the textarea. Whether it’s names, decisions, tasks, or fun challenges, the wheel will randomly pick one for you!

How It Works

  • Sign Up: Create an account to access your dashboard and start spinning. Please use a valid email for account recovery.
  • Login: Enter your username and password to access your spins and rewards.
  • Daily Spins: Each user receives 20 free spins per day. Spins reset automatically at midnight.
  • Enter Your Choices: Type any items you want into the textarea, each on a new line. These will appear on the wheel.
  • Spin the Wheel: Click the spin button to let the wheel randomly choose one of your entries.
  • View History: Every result is saved to your personal history so you can revisit past spins.
  • Account Issues: If you face any issues such as missing spins or login errors please contact support.

For additional help or questions, contact us via email. We aim to provide a fun and secure experience for all players.
