Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Spin to Win allows you to create personalized wheels using your own entries. We collect minimal data to offer a smooth and secure experience.

Information We Collect

  • Account Data: Username and email for login and account recovery.
  • Custom Entries & History: Your wheel entries and past results are saved only for your personal use.
  • Usage Activity: Spin results and interactions to ensure fair use and improve features.

How We Use Your Data

  • To display your custom history and saved entries.
  • To improve user experience and add new features.
  • To maintain fair functionality and prevent misuse.

Data Protection

We do not sell or share your information with third parties. All data is stored securely and used only within the platform to enhance your experience.

Cookies

Cookies may be used to remember your login session and preferences, ensuring a faster and smoother interaction.

Your Control

You may request data removal or account deletion at any time. For assistance, please contact support.
